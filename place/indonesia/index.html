This dataset gives a more extensive list of limited companies which includes both state-owned as well as private enterprises, and across sectors.

30 National Statistics

According to Law number 16 of 1997 on statistics, basic statistics and sectoral statistics are available for public use unless it is specified otherwise in prevailing legislation. Basic statistics being defined as statistics utilized for a broad range of (both government and community) purposes, which have cross-sectoral characteristics, are on a national and macro scale, and will be the responsibility of the Statistics Agency.
